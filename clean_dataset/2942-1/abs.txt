The ABSTRACT is to be in fully-justified italicized text, at the top of the left-hand column, below the author and affiliation information. Use the word ``Abstract'' as the title, in N-point Times, boldface type, centered relative to the column, initially capitalized. The abstract is to be in N-point, single-spaced type. Leave two blank lines after the Abstract, then begin the main text. Look at previous NDV abstracts to get a feel for style and length.
